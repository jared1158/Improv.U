Positivty Jar

Life is unproportionally full of the dark and twisties, as it is of the sappy and happy.
I am a believer of practicing what one preaches, and can attest that a positivty jar
truly does improve one's quality of life. Positivity jars requires you to focus on the positive,
in your life and let go of the negative through positive affirmation onto multicolored sticky notes.

Steps

  • Step 1: Obtain the following items: Mason Jar , multi-colored sticky notes , pens *feel free to substitute what you see fit for any of the items above* :-)
  • Step 2: AT LEAST once a week, write down ONE positive thing that occured onto a sticky note.
  • Step 3: Next, fold sticky note, and then place into jar.
  • Step 4: Whenever you're feeling low, open up your jar and reflect on what is good.
    Remember: “Trees that are slow to grow bear the best fruit.” ― Molière
